Iboto.ph

The Philippine digital sphere has become a political weapon.

For the Philippine midterms, I conceptualized and launched into fruition a project to campaign for and spread voter information about progressive Philippine senatorial and partylist candidates. We reached millions of Filipinos across a month of pre-election, and election vote counting, with our materials directly shared over >100k times. Today, Iboto.ph is gearing up to be a go-to platform for disruptive digital grassroots campaigning for the 2022 elections.

Roles Lead engineer, design, and project growth with a remote team Methods Rapid research and inquiry, user journey evaluations, remote usability studies, web development, visual design.
