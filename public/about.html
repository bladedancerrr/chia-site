About Chia

I'm Chia, a CS and Art rising junior at Yale from Manila.

As a technologist, I seek to question systems at scale—radical spaces that empower creation drive me. I love exploring the potential of the web to nurture creators, and building individual experiences that fundamentally change the way you see the world.

I founded Developh to disrupt tech in Southeast Asia, serving 80,000+ students as we build tech-driven ventures for good. I serve as President at UX Society Yale, build for a progressive Philippines at Iboto.ph, and design for a connected Philippines at LAB.PH.

I've designed for collective action at the Yale Women's Center, Manila Pride March, games, literary publications, newsrooms under fire by the national government, and more. I also do creative code, scout for the Southeast Asian tech ecosystem.

As an occasional speaker, I was the 2018 AnitaB.org Student of Vision at the GHC keynote stage—speaking in frequent workshops at Developh to arenas of 20,000. Last year, I spoke in three continents.

You'll see me attending shows, updating Wikipedia, worldbuilding, collecting vinyl, reading on game design, playing incrementals, and watching three-hour long movies.
