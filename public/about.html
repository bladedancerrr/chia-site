Chiara Amisola

Chiara is a nineteen-year-old student at Yale studying computer science & art. From Manila, ever since she was young, all she wanted to do was create.

What best describes what I want to become is a creative technologist—I'm a designer & coder interested in unlocking the potential of the web as a medium to tell stories, bridge people together, and empower creation.

Currently, I run Developh nurturing student founders in Southeast Asia while building social impact products, and User Experience Society Yale building human-centric design & engineering leaders at Yale. My early 2020 projects are editing a newsletter for Philippine tech at Technology.ph, curating experimental tech at the Yale CCAM, and working on a femtech project for Filipinas.

I'm building web experiences, games, writing studies and/or essays on life and creation. I build to impact. HELLO@CHIA.DEV—let's talk about the web and change.
